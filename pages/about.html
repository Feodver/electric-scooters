This website is dedicated to electric scooters and was developed by Fedor Mayorov, a student at the RTU MIREA. The development of this site started because of the passion for electric scooters and the desire to share this interest with others.
When creating this website, Fyodor was inspired by the designs of major companies such as Apple and Xiaomi. He aimed to create a convenient and modern interface that would be understandable and enjoyable for all visitors. In addition, an important goal was to make the site informative and useful for anyone interested in electric scooters.
